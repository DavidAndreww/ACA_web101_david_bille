Hi, I'm Kristi!

I started a new chapter in my life with my two amazing kids. It's more like a new book. A new beginning for us. I moved to a big city, trying new things and taking a leap of faith. This is a place of grace. I hope you feel encouraged and join me in taking a leap of faith!

A bit of history:
The past 12 years I've lived in a small country town. Met my ex and we have two wonderful kids - a sweet boy named Connor and a joyful daughter named Scotty. My ex and I were together for 10 years, but ended our relationship over a year ago. Through the storm, God never left my side. I got aptized, tried new hobbies like woodworking, fishing, hiking, dating and moved to a HUGE city where I continue to try new things! You can say I'm living by that saying "YOLO", but in a Godlly way!

I'm loving life, loving this new beginning and I'm happier than I've been in a while. I hope you feel inspired by my blog to try new things in your own life!

I can do all things through Him who strengthens me - Philippians 4:13 and so can you! Thanks for checking out my journey! I can go on, but I'll let you read the juicy stuff about my life in my blog.
