Hi! I’m Kristi. I started a new chapter in my life with my two amazing kids. It's more like a new book. A new beginning for us. I moved to a big city, trying new things, and taking a leap of faith. This is a place of grace. I hope you feel encouraged and join me in taking a leap of faith! A bit of history: The past 12 years I’ve lived in a small country town. Met my ex and we have two wonderful kids – a sweet boy named Connor and a joyful daughter named Scotty. My ex and I were together for 10 years, but ended our relationship over a year ago. Through the storm, God never left my side. I got baptized, tried new hobbies like woodworking, go fishing on my own, hiking, dating, moved to a HUGE city, and continue to try new things! You can say I’m living by that saying, YOLO. I like to say YOLO, but in a Godly way :) I'm loving life, loving this new beginning, and happier than I've been in a while. I hope you feel inspired by my blog to try new things from woodworking, healthy living, new fashion, etc! ​ I can do all things through Him who strengthens me – Philippians 4:13, and you can, too! Thanks for checking out my journey! I can go on, but I’ll let you read the juicy stuff about my life in my blog. Feel free to contact me! Like, follow, tweet, snap, etc. ;)
